Piazzo

Experience more memorable moments with your friends.

Piazzo let's you connect with friends right now to gather more friends and do more stuff.

Login with Facebook

We don't post to your wall or do any nasty stuff without you doing it, we just want to know your city and your FB friends so you can go out and experience more with your friends

How to

  • Click "I'm up for something".
  • You'll see your friends that are also up for something right now within a 20km radius.
  • You can join a chat with anyone on the list or call them directly
  • Chat and decide or call and go do stuff.

When to

  • Find more friends for lunch
  • Invite more people for drinks
  • Your date stood you up? Find a friend to complain to.
  • Foreign city? (you can see all of your FB friends who live in the same city where you are now)

Mechanics of Piazzo

  1. Your status is automatically cancelled in 1 hour.
  2. You can't cancel your status within 10 minutes.

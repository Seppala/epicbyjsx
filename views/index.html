Do you want to meet your friends more often?

Of course you do.

Piazzo makes it easy. Just set your status to "up for something" and give an optional message of what you have in mind. Your friends will see your status and you will see theirs. Then just call them up!

There are two things to know about how Piazzo works:

  1. Your status is automatically cancelled in 1 hour.
  2. You can't cancel your status within 10 minutes.
